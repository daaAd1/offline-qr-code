In contrast to many other add-ons, which use Google Web APIs for generating QR codes, this add-on works completely offline. This QR code generator puts your privacy first! Effectively, the add-on prevents any web connection for itself, so it cannot even technically contact the web. Thanks to the linked manifest.json you can also easily verify, that this claim is true. It works offline! Always. So easy, you can use it with one click! It has a radically simple, yet powerful user interface, allowing you to tweak many details in the settings, but being lightweight when you use it. A powerful usability improvement is the feature to just resize the QR code with your mouse by dragging and dropping. Being lightweight and thus having a small size it is also fast and easy to install – even on mobile connections. It is inspired by the old Offline QR code generator add-on for Firefox 56 and lower. Generate a variety of QR codes! Generate colored QR codes, save QR codes or just simple black/white ones to send a tab or other text from your desktop to your mobile! You have the choice! Your QR code will only be one click away and you can an instantly start typing to change the QR code text! Your QR code will be generated automatically. More features
  • Puts privacy first! Privacy should be the default, so it is generating QR codes offline.
  • Follows Firefox Photon Design to integrate smoothly into the design of your Firefox.
  • It's super simple and intuitively usable!
  • Uses up-to-date, great, and customizable QR code libraries to generate the QR codes.
  • You can generate and save QR codes as SVG or PNG image (Canvas)!
  • Lets you configure the size, color other aspects of the QR code.
  • Complete internationalization possible.
  • Complete Unicode/UTF-8/Emoji support.
  • Looks good on desktop and mobile devices, i.e. it is responsive!
  • Translated in English and German already. Contribute your own language!
  • Compatible with Firefox for Android
  • Settings are synced across devices.
  • Settings can be managed by your administrator. (work in progress)
